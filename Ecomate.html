The Challenge

Why E-Waste Is a
Growing Crisis

Improper disposal of electronics leaks toxic materials, destroys ecosystems, and wastes valuable resources that could be recovered and reused.

☠️
Toxic Chemical Pollution
Lead, mercury, and cadmium from discarded electronics seep into soil and water supplies, causing long-term environmental damage.
🏥
Serious Health Risks
Exposure to hazardous materials from informal e-waste handling causes neurological damage, respiratory illness, and cancer.
🔐
Data Security Breaches
Discarded devices with unwiped data expose personal and corporate information, leading to identity theft and privacy violations.
🗑️
Landfill Overflow
E-waste is the fastest-growing waste stream globally. Without proper systems, valuable resources are buried in landfills forever.
